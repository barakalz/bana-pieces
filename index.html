About Me

I'm originally from Cincinnati, Ohio (Who Dey!). While attending the University of Cincinnati for undergrad studies in Operations Management, I completed 4 co-op rotations in consulting & product management. Through those co-op experiences, I discovered that combining a focus on efficiency and performance with quantitative data analysis can supercharge companies.

As a result, I pursued a Masters of Science in Business Analytics at UC with my fifth year. The degree taught me vital skills in statistical modeling and analysis. Upon completion of the degree, I relocated to Chicago for my first job as a strategic finance analyst at Riverside Insights, a company that specializes in k-12 assessments for benchmark, gifted and talented, and clinical diagnostic applications. I hope to continue to create value for companies as I pursue my professional journey by combining my skills in analytics, finance, and operations for a unique and holistic business intelligence perspective.

In my free time, I love learning new songs on the electric guitar, weightlifting, and exploring restaurants and bars in the Chicago area. I also love to ski (both water and snow), travel, watch Cincinnati sports, and cook great meals.

Thank you for your interest in my journey! You can find my assorted contact information on my resume as well as within the links on the left banner of the page.
